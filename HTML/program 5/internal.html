MCA

MCA is a three year (Six Semester) regular program recognized by CUSAT. All the seats of MCA are filled through the qualifiers of CUSAT Common Admission Test (CAT) examination. The Eligibility of admission is any Degree with Mathematics as one of the main or subsidiary subjects or graduate in Electronics / Computer Science / Computer Applications / Engineering / Technology with an aggregate of 55% marks.
